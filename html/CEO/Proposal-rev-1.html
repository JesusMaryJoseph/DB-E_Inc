A) In 1971 Texas Instruments(TI) introduced the 74LS__ series of Integrated Circuits(ICs).

B) In 1975 MOS Technology introduced the 6502 8-bit microprocessor.

C) In 1977 Companies like Apple, Radio Shack, Commodore introduced the first Personal Computers. The magazine Byte introduced them as:
"1977 trinity": Commodore PET, Apple II, and TRS-80 Model I

  1. Form a Company that will:
    1. Simulate Commodore, Apple and Radio Shack
    2. Design and Build an 8-bit Computer from the 74LS__ series of Integrated Circuits(ICs).
    3. NOTE: This predates 1975 so this 8-bit Computer will not have the capabilities of say the Apple II, TRS-80, or PET; however, the fundamental operations of the computer will be very similar.

  2. Structure of the Company:
    1. CEO (mandatory)
    2. Four Main Departments (mandatory)
      1. Engineering
      2. Drafting
      3. Technician
      4. Computer
    3. Possible additional Departments (some could be combined)
      1. Education
      2. Media
      3. Quality Control
      4. Finance

  3. Personnel:
    1. Six to Eight Students from Eagle Butte High School in Eagle Butte, SD
    2. Six to Eight Students from Chesterton Academy High School in Albuquerque, NM

  4. When:
    1. May 30 to Aug 2, 2025

  5. Where:

  6. General Time Frame:

        2. Software Architecture also know as “Instruction Set Architecture”(ISA) (see Appendix B)
      3. Sample Project: BCD to 7-Segment Decoder
        1. Introduction to Binary #’s, the 7-Segment Display and the 74LS47/48 IC Data Sheet.
        2. Each person will have the opportunity to experience the Engineering, Drafting and Building phases.
        3. Level 2: (optional) (Determined by the Algebraic abilities of the students.) Repeat the above without the 74LS47/48 IC. Given the Input & Output, Truth Tables will be created, from which the Engineer(student) can, using Boolean Algebra, come up with their design for the decoder. (see Tutorial – Truth Tables located in the Engineering Dept). NOTE: many solutions are possible, some being better than others.
